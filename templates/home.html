ABOUT US

Girls Who Code at the University of Michigan-Dearborn is a student-led organization dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology. We provide a supportive community and resources for women interested in computer science, offering workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities to empower the next generation of female technologists.
