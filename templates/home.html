What We Do

More than coding

Our chapter is built around the idea that women in tech deserve more than a seat in the room. We create opportunities to learn, lead, mentor, and build lasting connections with a community that believes in each other.

Technical Growth

Workshops in web development, AI, cybersecurity, app development, and more.

Professional Development

Panels, networking events, resume support, and career-building opportunities.

Mentorship & Outreach

Programming designed to support both college members and the next generation of girls in tech.

Signature Initiative

TechHer Conference

TechHer is one of our signature outreach experiences, bringing together students, mentors, and educators for a day centered on exploration, empowerment, and belonging in computer science and engineering.
