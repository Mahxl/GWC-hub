About Us

Girls Who Code at the University of Michigan-Dearborn is a student-led organization dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology. We create a supportive community through technical workshops, professional development, mentorship, and outreach.

Board Members & Interns Who Make the Magic Happen

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower women in tech by creating spaces where students can grow technically, professionally, and personally.

What We Do

  • Technical workshops in web development, AI, cybersecurity, and more
  • Professional development events and networking opportunities
  • Mentorship for K-12 students and college members
  • Community-building events that strengthen sisterhood
